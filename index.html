Welcome to Kampala Jellitone Suppliers

ABOUT US

Kampala Jellitone Suppliers Ltd is a Manufacturing Company based in Nateete, a suburb of Kampala, Uganda. We supply three unique eco-friendly products to the Ugandan market – Roasted Coffee, Biomass Briquettes and Briquette Stoves. All products are the highest quality available on the market. Our energy-saving fuel and stoves are designed by Professional Engineers, all of whom have an extensive knowledge of the biomass fuels. We are dedicated to ensuring that through our business activities, the rich environment of Uganda is cultivated and sustained.






WHY CHOOSE US

Award Winning

Our unique Biomass Briquette Fuel is a World Class energy saving product. We became a finalist winner of the International Ashden Award for Sustainable Energy in 2009. We were presented the award by Prince Charles at Blah, London

Eco-Friendly

Using our briquettes reduces the pressure on wood resources and thus reduces deforestation, which is a serious and growing problem- particularly around Kampala

Certified

Kampala Jellitone Suppliers is a certified member of the Pro-Biodiversity Conservationists in Uganda, and therefrore holds their official stamp of approval

OUR TEAM

Haji Abasi Kazibwe Musisi Managing Director

The company was founded by Haji Abasi Kazibwe Musisi, who is an experienced banker, formerly working for Standard Chartered Bank Uganda. He co-ordinates all business activities and oversees the research at the Fuels from Waste Research Centre.


Paul Okello Hatuba General Manager

Paul has been working for KJS Ltd over seven years, supervising the management staff within the coffee roasting and energy stove business units. He has a degree in Commerce majoring in Procurement and a diploma in Education. He has an extensive knowledge of energy and biomass fuels having regularly attended training events and workshops.


Ronald Ssebaale Lukoda Finance Manager

Ronald has been an employee of KJS Ltd since 2007, acquiring the position as finance manager in 2012. He has a degree in Commerce, a diploma in Business Studies and certificate in Computer Science. Attending international and national energy related seminars, Ronald has a proficient knowledge of biomass fuels. Ronald supports BEETA, a non-governmental organisation that encourages the networking of Ugandan companies that use biomass energy technologies.


Charlotte Najjuma Marketing and Sale Manager of Briquette

As the most recent member of the KJS Ltd management team, Charlotte oversees the sales and marketing for our biomass briquette product. She has a wealth of business experience having worked for Hotel Muzza, Kampala. Moreover, she has previously established her own company, marketing and selling agricultural products.


Daniel Kakyengi Construction Engineer

Daniel has eleven year experience engineering and constructing energy saving stoves for KJS Ltd. He has an advanced diploma in Technical Studies. He manages the construction team at KJS Ltd, who build and maintain the energy saving stoves. He also equips the customer’s with the knowledge of how to use the stoves.
